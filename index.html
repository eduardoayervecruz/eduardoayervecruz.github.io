Eduardo Ayerve Cruz

I am currently a AI & Robotics Researcher at the company Tommoro Robotics in Seoul, South Korea. I graduated from Seoul National University, where I majored in Computer Science and Engineering. I was a recipient of the Global Korea Scholarship for the period 2020~2025 and also a part of the network of High Performance High Schools for the Gifted in Peru (COAR in Spanish) in high-school. During my time in university, I developed a special interest in the AI field, having participated in various projects related to it, such as computer vision, natural language processing, deep learning for robotics, transformers and optimization of neural network structures. My career in AI started when I became a research intern in the RUBIS lab led by Prof. Chang-Gun Lee, where I collaborated in the development of a Text-To-Speech model specialized in the korean language, which was used in a project that provided mental health assistance to students in South Korea. Later, I became an intern at the BioIntelligence Lab from Prof. Byoung-Tak Zhang, where I worked in two projects: first, the development of a voice control interface for robot manipulation for the South Korean robots RB-Y1 and AI Worker, and in the development of a integrated console for training and inferencing.
