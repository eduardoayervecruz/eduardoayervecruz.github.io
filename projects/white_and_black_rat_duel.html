Game Project

The White and Black Rat Duel

3D Platformer Adventure

About the Game

Embark on a culinary adventure in this charming 3D platformer where you play as a white rat competing against a black rat in an epic cooking showdown. Journey through imaginative kitchen-inspired worlds—from bustling orchards to a massive chopping board to stretches of dough—gathering ingredients, overcoming challenges, and battling quirky opponents.

Pick up special abilities to gain the upper hand, explore creative environments, and prepare for an unforgettable showdown against your rival. With accessible gameplay and charming visuals, this game offers fun for players of all skill levels.

Features

  • Creative Levels: Explore diverse kitchen-themed worlds with unique challenges
  • Ingredient Collection: Gather fresh ingredients throughout your journey
  • Power-ups & Abilities: Unlock special abilities to enhance your gameplay
  • Quirky Enemies: Battle charming opponents and overcome obstacles
  • Epic Boss Battle: Face off in an unforgettable final showdown

Play the Game

Download and play the game on your macOS device. The game is built with modern graphics and smooth gameplay for an immersive experience.
